About Infrared

Infrared is an open-source web proxy built for speed and privacy. It uses Ultraviolet, Scramjet, Epoxy, Libcurl, and Wisp to route traffic securely through your browser. Join the community on Discord.

Why do some sites not work?

Common reasons: the proxy doesn't support that site, server load, site-side issues, or security flags. Try switching the proxy engine or transport in Proxy & Browser settings.

I found a bug

Open an issue on GitLab or a ticket on Discord.

How do I change my theme?

Go to Settings → Themes and click any theme card. The change applies instantly and is saved automatically.
